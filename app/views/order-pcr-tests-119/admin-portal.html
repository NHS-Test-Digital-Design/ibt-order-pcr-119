PCR Tests

Most sensitive type of test. It must be processed at a lab.

Get a PCR test kit

  • The person has not given sample yet
  • Test kit is delivered to the person's home and returned to a lab by post
  • The sample must be processed at a lab
  • Test results are usually available within 72 hours. It could take longer than this, but 119 are only able to investigate after 5 days.

  • The sample has already been taken
  • The sample is at the lab for processing and the person needs their result

Rapid lateral flow tests

Lower sensitivity with faster results.

Get a rapid lateral flow test kit

  • Test kits are delivered to a person's home
  • The test kit stays with the person and they see the result of the sample themselves
  • It takes approximately 15-30 minutes to get a result
