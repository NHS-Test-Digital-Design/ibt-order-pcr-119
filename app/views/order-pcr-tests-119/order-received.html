What happens next

Your COVID-19 test kit should arrive in the next 2 days.

The test kit contains a swab, instructions on how to use it and return packaging.

You'll receive a confirmation email soon. There's important information in your confirmation email about how to complete your test. Follow the instructions carefully to make sure you receive your result.

What to do when you receive your test kit

Follow the instructions in your test kit. The instructions will tell you:

  • how and when to do the swab test
  • how to return your sample

Instructions in Braille, audio or large print

Royal National Institute of Blind People (RNIB) can send you Braille, audio or large print instructions. Order alternative format instructions online or call 0303 123 9999 (Monday to Friday 8am to 8pm and Saturday 9am to 1pm).

Getting help

You can call 119 (free from mobiles and landlines) if you need extra support.

Lines are open:

  • Monday to Friday (including bank holidays), 8am to 6pm
  • Saturday and Sunday, 9am to 1pm

SignVideo is a free online British Sign Language interpreter service for 119.

If you're worried

If your symptoms gets worse, or you do not get better after 7 days, use the NHS 111 online service.
